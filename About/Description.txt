  Adds a makeable banana suit to RimWorld for use in your colonies and scenarios.
  
  The Banana Suit provides insolation, covers clothing, and is great at parties.
  Minimum Technology: Medieval
  Required Research: Complex clothing
  Armor: 30%
  Insulation Cold: 40%
  Insulation Heat: 20%
  + Can be used as ideology apparel
  + Social Impact Bonus: +15%
  + Kid banana suits are available for Biotech
  Costs: 80 Leather / Fabric, 1 Dye
